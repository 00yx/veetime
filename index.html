Will you be my Valentine?

Forever and Always, Manjot

From the moment our worlds intertwined,
You captured my heart, my soul, my mind.
No words could ever express,
The love I feel for you, how much I’m blessed.

You are beauty in its truest form,
Not just in looks, but in the special warmth
Of your heart, your mind, your gentle brace,
The way love shines upon your face.

Your smile is brighter than the morning sun,
A light that can’t be outdone.
Your laughter’s a melody, soft and sweet,
A sound that makes my heart skip so many beats.

But it’s not just beauty my eyes can see,
It’s the way your soul speaks to me.
Your kindness, your strength, the way that you care,
You’re rare, you’re precious, beyond compare.

You’re not just someone I love,
You’re my peace, my stars above.
You’re my anchor, strong and true,
And there’s nothing in this world I wouldn’t do for you.

I vow to cherish, to love, to protect,
To never neglect, never ever forget
That you are worthy, more than you know,
And I’ll show you, through actions, not just words alone.

I won’t give you less than you deserve,
I’ll give you my all, my heart, my world.
Each day, I’ll strive to be the best I can be,
Not just for me, but for you, for we.

I’ll better myself, I’ll learn, I’ll grow,
So our love can flourish, strong and whole.
I’ll be patient, I’ll listen, I’ll stand by your side,
With loyalty and love that never subsides.

No distance, no hardship, no trial, no test,
Could make me love you any less.
I’ll never leave, I’ll never stray,
My heart is yours, now and always.

Through every storm, through every season,
I’ll be there, and you are the reason.

You are my everything, my heart, my home,
And with you, I am never alone.
So on this day, and all days too,
Know this, my love, I love you.
